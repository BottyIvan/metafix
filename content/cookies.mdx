# Cookie Policy

This page describes the use of cookies and similar technologies on the site/app.

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small files saved on your device that enable the proper functioning and improvement of the service.

## How we use cookies

We only use technical cookies required for platform functionality.

If tracking and ads are enabled (Google Tag Manager / Google Ads), third-party
cookies may be set for measurement and ad personalization, according to the
choices collected by your consent system (CMP).

## Managing cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling
cookies may affect the availability of some features.

For questions about cookies, contact info@tuodominio.com.
