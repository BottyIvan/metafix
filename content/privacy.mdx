# Privacy Policy

This policy explains how user data is handled when using MetaFix.

## Data Controller

The data controller is the service developer.
For any questions, contact: info@tuodominio.com.

## Data Collected

MetaFix does not require registration and does not collect personally
identifiable information. Uploaded files are processed locally in the browser
and are not uploaded to app servers.

## Cookies and Tracking

The service uses technical cookies required by the hosting platform. When
tracking and ads are enabled via Google Tag Manager / Google Ads, third-party
tools can be used for measurement and ad delivery based on consent
preferences collected by your CMP.

## Purpose of Processing

- provide the web application
- ensure operational reliability and security

## Legal Basis

Processing is based on delivering the requested service and the controller's
legitimate interest in infrastructure security.

## Data Retention

Technical platform data is retained only for the time strictly required for
technical and security obligations.

## User Rights

You can request information or exercise your rights by contacting
info@tuodominio.com.

## Updates

This policy may be updated. Changes will be published on this page.
